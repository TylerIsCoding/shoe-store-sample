About Us

Located at 123 Main Street in Cambridge MA; Silverpoint has been a staple of the local sneaker and skater scene. Established with the intention of providing the latest kicks and styles to the locals, the doors opened on August 10th, 1994. Our mission statement is the bring a fun, friendly, and playful atmosphere for people to kick back and shop.

Silverpoint has partnerships with sneaker companies such as Nike, Adidas, DC, New Balance, and more so we always have a great selection of kicks to fit your style. We regularly get shipments of the latest sneaker drops so make sure to stop by. We also started branching out from sneakers into watches, t-shirts, jackets and more in recent years.

FAQ

Q: How long does shipping take?

A: We try to ship our items as fast as possible. Usually we ship next day (excluding orders placed on holidays).

Q: How are your items shipped?

A: Item's are shipped via FedEx Express 2-day. We use our own packaging to make sure the item arrives safely. If there are any issues please contact support at support@silverpoint.com.

Q: What if my shoes and/or clothes don't fit?

A: Go ahead and send a support email or give us a call! We'll start processing a return and even email you a return label! We're currently working on an online return system. Stay tuned!

Q: Can we come visit you in person?
